# Produce your book

When your book is written, checked, and described, you produce the file you'll share. The main output is an EPUB — your whole book in the standard ebook format. You can also save the book, or a single chapter, as a PDF.

## Package and export an EPUB

Click _Package EPUB_{.ui} at the foot of the sidebar. SEED.html assembles your chapters, styles, images, cover, and metadata into a single `.epub` file and opens the _Publish_{.ui .icon-export} view, where the finished book is listed.

From there, click _Download_{.ui} to save the `.epub` to your device. That file is your book — ready to read in any reading app, or to share. And because it's a SEED EPUB, it carries its own source, so you (or anyone you pass it to) can open it back in SEED.html and keep editing. Package again whenever you've made changes, to produce an updated file.

## Save as PDF

You can also save your book as a PDF — a fixed, printable copy, handy for proof-reading on paper or giving to someone who just wants to print it. A PDF isn't a substitute for the EPUB: it doesn't reflow or adapt to the reader, and it can't be reopened in SEED.html to edit. Packaging an EPUB, above, is how you produce the book proper.

Open _Settings_{.ui .icon-gear} and find its **PDF** section. Set the **Page size** and **Margin**, choose whether to **Include page numbers** and a cover page, then click _Generate PDF_{.ui} to produce a PDF of the whole book.

To save just one chapter — for a handout or a quick sample — open it, choose the _PDF_{.ui} preset in the preview header (the print preview from _Preview your book_), and click the _Chapter PDF_{.ui} button that appears.
